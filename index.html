What is Crafting for Cancer

We are an organization that reaches out to cancer patients while acquiring and maintaining the unique skill of crocheting. Members learn how to crochet beanies which are donated to Jessie Rees Foundation’s Brave Beanie program. This foundation give these beanies are put into Jessie’s Joy Jars which are given to kids fighting cancer.
